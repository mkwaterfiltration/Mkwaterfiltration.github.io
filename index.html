Our Story

Founded in Bhopal, MK Water Filtration was born from a vision to provide Central India with reliable and technologically advanced water treatment solutions. As a leading prime Manufacturer, Exporter, Trader, Supplier, and Service Provider, we are committed to tackling the region's diverse water challenges for industrial, commercial, and residential clients.

Our Vision

To be a socially responsible and environmentally friendly water treatment brand making a difference in people's lives by providing the best water treatment products and service.

Our Mission

Our mission is to improve the quality of water that was wasted in the past, maintain the quality of water we have in the present, and work on the future requirement of drinking water with the help of our innovative products and esteemed services.

Our Product Catalogue

A comprehensive look at our high-quality water treatment solutions.

Commercial RO Plant

We offer a wide range of commercial water treatment equipment that purifies water by deploying advanced RO technology that meets the challenging environment of industries.

Industrial RO Plant

Backed by our in-depth industry expertise, we are offering an unmatched range of Industrial RO Water Plants with exclusive designs, performance, and long service life.

STP & ETP

Effective and economical plants to remove contaminants from wastewater. We manufacture STP plants from 1 KLD to 900 KLD and ETP plants from 1 KLD to 500 KLD.

Chiller Plant

A Chiller plant is essential to remove heat from liquid, which can then be used to cool equipment by circulating it through a heat exchanger.

Water ATM

Providing safe and affordable water at the press of a button. We manufacture Water ATMs from 100 LPH to 10000 LPH capacity.

Bottling Plant

Complete bottling solutions, including PET blowing machines, automatic rinsing, filling, and capping machines, UV systems, and more for beverage distribution.

Water Cooler

We provide a variety of water coolers and dispensers, including point-of-use (POU) and bottled water coolers from top brands like Voltas and Bluestar.

Ultra Filtration System

A membrane filtration process where pressure leads to separation through a semipermeable membrane, used to recycle and reuse water.

Water Treatment Plant (WTP)

Any process that improves water quality for a specific end-use. We provide plants from 500 LPH to 300,000 LPH in MS, SS, and FRP.
